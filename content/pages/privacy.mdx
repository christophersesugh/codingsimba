# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 15, 2025**

Welcome to Coding Simba. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, services, and website.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect:

- **Name and contact information** (email address, phone number)
- **Account credentials** (username, password)
- **Profile information** (bio, profile picture, preferences)
- **Payment information** (processed securely through third-party providers)
- **Communication preferences**

### 1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of our platform:

- **Device information** (IP address, browser type, operating system)
- **Usage patterns** (pages visited, time spent, features used)
- **Learning progress** (course completion, quiz scores, assignments)
- **Technical data** (error logs, performance metrics)

### 1.3 Content and Communications

- **Course submissions** and assignments
- **Forum posts** and community interactions
- **Support requests** and feedback
- **Communication with instructors** and other users

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Service Provision

- Provide access to courses, content, and learning materials
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Deliver personalized learning experiences
- Enable community features and peer interactions

### 2.2 Platform Improvement

- Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
- Develop new features and content
- Optimize platform performance and user experience
- Conduct research and analytics

### 2.3 Communication

- Send important updates about your account
- Provide customer support and technical assistance
- Share educational content and course updates
- Send marketing communications (with your consent)

## 3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 3.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who:

- Process payments and handle financial transactions
- Provide hosting and infrastructure services
- Deliver email and communication services
- Conduct analytics and research

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Respond to government requests

### 3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption** of data in transit and at rest
- **Secure authentication** and access controls
- **Regular security audits** and vulnerability assessments
- **Employee training** on data protection practices

### 4.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal information
- **Update** or correct inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and associated data
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Opt out** of marketing communications

### 5.2 Account Settings

You can manage your privacy preferences through your account settings:

- Update profile information
- Control communication preferences
- Manage data sharing settings
- Download your data

### 5.3 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Maintain your login session
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze platform usage
- Provide personalized content

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

## 6. International Data Transfers

### 6.1 Global Operations

Our services may be accessed from various countries. Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own.

### 6.2 Data Protection Standards

We ensure that international data transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

## 7. Children's Privacy

### 7.1 Age Requirements

Our platform is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### 7.2 Parental Consent

If you are under 18, you must have parental or guardian consent to use our services.

## 8. Third-Party Services

### 8.1 External Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

### 8.2 Social Media Integration

When you connect your social media accounts, those platforms may collect additional information according to their privacy policies.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

### 9.1 Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

- Posting the updated policy on our website
- Sending email notifications
- Displaying prominent notices on our platform

### 9.2 Continued Use

Your continued use of our services after policy changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
