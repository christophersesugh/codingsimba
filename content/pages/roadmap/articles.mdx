# Articles Section Development

## Overview

The articles section serves as the foundation of our learning platform, providing comprehensive written content that covers programming concepts, best practices, and industry insights.

## Key Features Implemented

### Content Management

- **Content Management System**: Sanity CMS with MDX support for content creation and management
- **MDX Support**: Full MDX support with custom components and syntax highlighting
- **Frontmatter System**: Structured metadata for articles including tags, categories, and SEO
- **Image Management**: CDN integration for optimized image delivery with fallback to placeholder images

### User Experience

- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with optimal reading experience
- **Table of Contents**: Auto-generated navigation for long articles
- **Reading Progress**: Visual progress indicator
- **Social Sharing**: Easy sharing to social media platforms

### SEO & Performance

- **Meta Tags**: Automatic generation of Open Graph and Twitter cards
- **Sitemap**: Dynamic sitemap generation for search engines
- **Performance**: Optimized loading with lazy loading and caching
- **Analytics**: Reading time estimates and engagement metrics

## Technical Implementation

### Backend Architecture

- **Database Schema**: Optimized for article comments and metrics storage
- **Search Functionality**: Full-text search with filters
- **Caching Strategy**: Turso (SQLite) with embedded replicas for high availability and zero latency on the edge

### Frontend Components

- **Article Cards**: Responsive grid layout with hover effects
- **Pagination**: Efficient pagination for large article collections
- **Filtering**: Advanced filtering by tags, categories, and date
- **Related Articles**: Powerful sanity queries to find related articles based on tags and categories
- **Article Comments**: Comment system with nested comments and reply functionality
- **Article Metrics**: Reading time estimates and engagement metrics
- **Article Reading Progress**: Visual progress indicator
- **Article Table of Contents**: Auto-generated navigation for long articles
- **Article Social Sharing**: Easy sharing to social media platforms
- **Newsletter Signup**: Newsletter signup form with double opt-in
